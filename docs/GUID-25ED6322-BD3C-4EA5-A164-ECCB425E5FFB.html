1.1.5 Getting Help

The TCP/IP Stack is supported through Microchip's standard support channels. If you encounter difficulties, you may submit ticket requests at Microchip Technical Support Portal.

The Microchip forums are also an excellent source of information, with a very lively community dedicated specifically to Ethernet and TCP/IP discussions at Microchip Community Forum.
